About Dirty Doggy

Family owned, El Paso rooted, built with hustle.

Dirty Doggy is powered by a local family that puts heart, pride, and real work into everything they serve. This isn’t just a food truck — it’s a family business built to feed the city they love.

Jimmy — The Dirty King @jimmy_da_tulip915

Born and raised on the east side of El Paso, Jimmy brings his passion for flavor straight to his city. From homemade scratch chili to the signature Dirty Doggy sauce, he puts love into it all. That same hustle, pride, and drive are what built Dirty Doggy into what it is today.

The Dirty Queen @jimswif3y

Also born and raised in El Paso, she brings heart, work ethic, and the warm energy behind the brand. With a smile and a welcoming spirit, she helps make sure every customer feels the family vibe behind Dirty Doggy.

The Dirty Pup @j1n0tfound

Their son and pride and joy, bringing hustle beyond his years. If he’s not on the court, he’s helping mom and dad at the truck, showing that Dirty Doggy is truly a family operation from top to bottom.

What It All Means Family owned. Community driven.

This is the family behind the business — an El Paso family serving their community and inviting people to come by, grab a bite, and be part of the Dirty Doggy story.
